What is Active 2D?

Along with the development of information technology, entertainment demand has also changed dramatically. From movies that only apply traditional techniques, now there are more 2D films and especially animated films, gradually converting to 2D as an inevitable trend. Therefore, following the hot career trend in the future, 2D Animation is considered a new industry that brings many job opportunities and challenges for digital artists in Vietnam.

 

 In the past, the process of creating animations was not easy and took a lot of time. Each frame in a scene will be hand drawn, then converted to a film (cel), then finally painted and colored. The completed cels are then arranged in sequence with the finished background and filmed; once per frame In recent years the methods of animation have become more diverse. Nowadays people can use a variety of ways to create cartoons, mainly by using computers to make the process of making movies cheaper and faster.

 

Attractive sound is the main ingredient that attracts the audience to find the animation. Voice actors, musicians, etc. can contribute their voices or music. Some recent animated movies require both vocal and musical talent to synchronize their sound into the film (and this is still guaranteed in films that are voiced to audiences across the globe. world). For the vast majority of animated movies these days, the tracks will be recorded in target market languages and animators require to synchronize their animation with the soundtrack.
